Who should you main?
There are plenty of characters to choose from in League. For each player, there is a character that fits their playstyle, and this website will help you find your ideal chcaracter.
The Basic Types

  • Tank

    Tanks are champions with high durability and crowd control. They help their teams stay alive by soaking up damage and immobilizing the enemy carries.

  • Fighter

    Fighters are champions with strong attacks and solid durability. They specialize in dueling other champions, and delivering decent damage in close range.

  • Mage

    Mages are champions who are frail but deliver high damage. They use their spells to do burst or consistent damage and typically stay behind their frontline.

  • Assassin

    Assassins are champions who are frail and deliver burst damage up close. They typically flank the enemy carries and attempt to disengage before they die.

  • Enchanter

    Enchanters are champions who give their carries shields, heals, and buffs. Their purpose to to keep their teammates alive by boosting their defenses.

  • Marksman

    Marksman are champions who deliver high damage per second with attacks from range. They are high value targets capable of killing even the tankiest tanks.
